Sushi Recipe

This Spicy Salmon Roll is perfectly balanced. It is creamy, crunchy, savory and spicy. Homemade sushi is very customizable, fun activity to do with anyone or even by yourself and very self fulfilling. There's just something about making your own homemade sushi and it's alot easier than you think too!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice vinegar
  • 1 2 inch square kombu
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp kosher salt
  • 2 cups short grain rice
  • 2 1/4 cups water
  • 1 cup kewpie mayo
  • 3 tbsp sriracha
  • 2 tsp gochujang
  • 1 thinly sliced green onion
  • Drops of sesame oil
  • 3 tbsp Spicy Mayo mix
  • 8 oz Kvaroy Arctic Salmon
  • 2 tsp furikake seasoning
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1-2 cucumber sticks per roll

Steps

  1. Slice the ingredients into thin strips. Even with larger rolls like this one, ingredients shouldn't be more than about Â¼-inch // 1 cm thick or it will quickly get hard to roll them up.
  2. Place a sheet of nori shiny-side down. The duller-looking, grippy inside face is the one that gets the ingredients. Use about half a cup of rice per roll and do your best to distribute it evenly over the nori. Leave a border at the far end for sealing.
  3. Layer the ingredients toward the side of the nori that's closest to you.
  4. Rolling is a two-part process. First you tuck in the ingredients and check to make sure they're secure. Then you seal the roll.
